IntelliProtector is a comprehensive multifunctional software protection solution providing a robust foundation for the monetization, protection, and license control of your intellectual property. With IntelliProtector, you can easily set up a secure software licensing system with a uniform interface for managing the activations, registrations, and renewals of your software. The main advantage of our solution is a compact yet functional design that easily allows you to control the licensing cycle of the product and track down the usage of the trial and licensed software copies.
